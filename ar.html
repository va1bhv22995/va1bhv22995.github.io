Virtual Reality

Virtual Reality (VR) is an alternative to our physical realm and is the pinnacle of virtualization. Here are some of my projects on it: .


Museum VR

A VR experience made for HTC Vive and using SteamVR libraries and APIs. Features a museum experience with artifacts from Ancient Asia, Ancient Egypt and Ancient Greek. All of them is visible in HD in Virtual Reality.





Augmented Reality

Augmented Reality (AR) is an enhanced version of our real world achieved through augmenting various visual enhancements onto the screen. I have made several projects on AR using Vuforia, ARFoundation, ARCore. .


Only Fast Shoot

An AR game made with unity using Vuforia where we have to shoot the spawning balloons in Virtual Reality..
